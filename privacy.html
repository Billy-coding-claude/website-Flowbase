Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

Flowbase ("I", "me") respects your privacy. This page explains what data this website handles and why. I only collect what I genuinely need to reply to you.

What I collect

Only the details you choose to send through the contact form: your name, business name, email address, and your message. I don't run advertising or analytics trackers.

How I use it

Your details are used solely to respond to your enquiry and, if we work together, to deliver the service. I never sell your data or share it for marketing.

The contact form

Form submissions are delivered to me by email, via the form-handling service Web3Forms, which processes the message only to forward it. If the form isn't connected yet, it instead opens your own email app with the message pre-filled.

Storage on your device

This site stores two small, non-tracking values in your browser's local storage: your language choice (NL/EN) and whether you've dismissed the privacy notice. These never leave your device and aren't used to identify you.

Fonts

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address as part of serving the font files. See Google's privacy policy for details.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you can ask to access, correct, or delete the data you've sent me at any time. Just email hello.flowbase@outlook.com and I'll take care of it.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email hello.flowbase@outlook.com.
