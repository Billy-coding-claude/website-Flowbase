About Flowbase

Premium websites for local businesses — without the agency price tag.

I started Flowbase because local businesses deserve better than a €5,000 agency invoice or a DIY template that looks like everyone else's. I'm a solo founder based in the Netherlands, which means you work directly with the person designing and building your site.
