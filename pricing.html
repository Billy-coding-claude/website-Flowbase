FAQ

Questions, answered honestly.

Still wondering about something? Email me — I'm happy to help, no pressure.

How long until my website is live?

Most sites go live in around two weeks once I have your content. Bigger builds with booking or logins take a little longer — you'll always get a clear timeline upfront.

Who owns the website and the content?

You do — completely. The site, the copy, the images, and the domain are yours. There's no lock-in and nothing held hostage.

What's included in the monthly care plan?

Hosting, security, backups, software updates, and small content tweaks — so your site stays fast, safe, and current without you having to think about it.

Do I have to write the text myself?

No. I draft the copy and structure based on our chat about your business — you just review and approve. Good writing is part of the job.

Can you also handle my social media?

Yes — that's the content side of Flowbase. Posts and reels, done for you every month, either on their own or bundled with your care plan.

How does payment work?

A deposit to get started and the rest on completion for the build. Monthly plans are billed month to month, and you can cancel whenever you like.
